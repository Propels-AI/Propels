Welcome to Propels!

Propels is a demo showroom that allows you to embed anywhere to capture leads!
  1. Click the extensions and pin Propels Demo Capturer.
  2. Go to the page you want to show. Click the Propels icon and hit Start Recording.
  3. Navigate like you're showing a customer in a video call. When you're finished, click the icon again and choose Stop Recording. We'll then open the editor.
